Young Gambling in Our Society

Every day in our modern society, children are being to encouraged to gamble in video games. This is encouraged through things such as loot boxes and loot crates, in games such as Fortnite and CS:GO. Children are encouraged to spend their parent's money to buy loot boxes, in the hopes of getting rare and expensive items that they can sell for a lot of money. In reality though, most of the time, people get items worth less than the cost of the case, encouraging the children to buy another case to make their money back.
