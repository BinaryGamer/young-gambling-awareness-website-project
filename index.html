Young Gambling in Our Society

Every day in our modern society, children are being encouraged to gamble in video games. This is encouraged through things such as loot boxes and loot crates, which are virtual boxes that people can buy, that can contain different items worth different values, for example, somebody could buy a $2 case, with the possibility of getting a $0.05 item in return, or a $100 item.

In games such as Fortnite and CS:GO. Children are encouraged to spend their parent's money to buy loot boxes, in the hopes of getting rare and expensive items that they can sell for a lot of money. In reality though, people mostly get items worth less than the cost of the case, encouraging the children to buy another case to make their money back.

This Problem has existed in many forms since the 1860s, the most prevelant form of this issue, use to be trading cards, such as baseball cards, where kids were encouraged to buy sets of cards, in the hopes of getting a rare card which they could sell for more money. Or trade for more cards.

Nowadays, these trading cards have been replaced with loot boxes and loot crates as the new generations' online form of gambling. This type of online gambling is hard to control, as children are able to purchase loot boxes without the knowledge of their family or parents.
