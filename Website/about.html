How can I help?

There are many ways that you can help in the fight against Targeted Child Gambling. You can help raise awareness of the harm the child gambling does to children, and warn friends and family about the dangers of letting their children spend money on purchasing loot boxes in games.

You can also help, by boycotting games which contain in-app purchases, like loot crates.
